Suna dubios?

Board game design November 2023

Welcome to the world of "Suna dubios?" - a captivating trivia board game designed to challenge players' wit and ability to bluff. From the intricately crafted trivia cards to the unique user cards and the stylish game box, every element of this game is a testament to my passion for design. The trivia questions, carefully curated to be the weirdest and funniest snippets of knowledge, add a delightful twist to the gaming experience. Players must navigate through a sea of facts, some accompanied by intriguing explanations, adding an extra layer of enjoyment. Stay tuned for expansions and bonus cards, promising even more excitement in the near future.
